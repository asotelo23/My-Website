Aydan Sotelo

Bad Bunny (Benito Antonio Martinez Ocasio) is a Puerto Rican Grammy-winner, rapper, singer, and producer. He was born in San Juan and raised in Vega Baja, Puerto Rico. He started rising in 2016 from SoundCloud. He has also just won album of the year during the 2026 Grammy's. His album is the first all spanish album to win album of the year.
